# Business Strategy Review

Execute a comprehensive business strategy review across market, product, metrics, and growth.

## Usage

`/business-review <focus>`

## Focus Areas

- `market` - Market opportunity and competitive analysis
- `product` - Product positioning and roadmap
- `metrics` - Business metrics and KPIs
- `growth` - Growth strategy and scaling
- `full-review` - Complete business audit

## Examples

```
/business-review market
/business-review product
/business-review metrics
/business-review growth
/business-review full-review
```

## Review Workflow

### Market Analysis
- Define market size (TAM/SAM/SOM)
- Analyze competitive landscape
- Assess market trends
- Identify market gaps

### Product Strategy
- Review value proposition
- Analyze product positioning
- Plan feature roadmap
- Define product/market fit metrics

### Financial Metrics
- Evaluate unit economics
- Calculate CAC and LTV
- Assess profitability
- Plan financial forecasts

### Growth Strategy
- Define growth levers
- Plan customer acquisition
- Identify retention opportunities
- Design scaling roadmap
